The TechSphere Technologies Story

Started With a Problem. Scaling Into an Ecosystem.

TechSphere Technologies is a modern African software company building scalable mobile apps, SaaS platforms, AI-powered systems, and digital solutions for businesses and startups. We started with a simple observation: African businesses were underserved by technology. The tools available were either too expensive, not mobile-friendly, or built without understanding local context.

So we started building. First came OwoTrack — a business management app for the everyday Nigerian entrepreneur tracking revenue on paper and WhatsApp. Then HeartLink for meaningful social connections. Then FixNG for the artisans who power Nigerian homes.

Each product came from observing real problems. Each is built with the same conviction: Africa deserves technology built by Africans, for Africans.
