How FixNG Works

Book a professional artisan in under 5 minutes — from discovery to job completion.

1

Search For a Service

Open the app and search for the service you need — plumber, electrician, mechanic, and more — filtered by your location.

2

Compare & Choose

Browse verified artisan profiles, check ratings, reviews, pricing, and availability. Choose the best professional for your job.

3

Book & Confirm

Schedule the job at your preferred time. Get instant confirmation and real-time tracking as the artisan arrives.

4

Pay & Rate

Pay securely in-app after job completion. Rate your experience and help others find quality artisans.
